Executive, Marketing

Synesis IT Ltd.

Vacancy

01

Job Context

Synesis IT now invites applications from candidates who are talented and maintaining high ethical standard for the post of Executive, Marketing. The candidates who possess self driven, innovative attitude with problem solving skills will discover their right environment and find fast track career growth in Synesis IT.

Job Description / Responsibility

• Develop and implement marketing plan and take corrective action as per market feedback;

• Plan and prepare advertising and promotional material to increase sales of products or services, working with clients, company officials,and advertising agencies;

• Maintain brand integrity across products, advertising, and all other internal and external communication;

• Maintain relationship with external vendors for branding programs to create balance between cost & impact;

• Engage customers through social media as like Facebook or LinkedIn &

• Frequent visits for exiting and perspective clients to acquire /secure/enhance business in the assigned territory;

• Management of Promotional tools and materials;

• Writing internal and external letters and applications both in Bangla and English format;

• Maintain relationship with Mobile operators, Government and Stakeholders, prepare product contents and brochures and communicate with Stakeholders and coordinate with accounts for timely payments of external and internal used for branding;

• Generate innovative ideas and writing script, stories, article, press release as well as blogs.

 

Job Nature

Full-time

Educational Requirements

• Must have BBA / MBA in Marketing from any reputed University;

Job Requirements

• Age 25 to 30 year(s)

• Experience: 1 to 2 year(s)

• Freshers can also apply;

• The applicants should have experience in the following area(s): Brand Planning/Development, Business Development, Advertising & Promotion, Corporate Marketing.

• Must possess strong management skills and ability to work on tight timelines;

• Strong interpersonal & communication skills, works well with team members;

• Must have the idea about social media;

• Must have the computer literacy as like MS Word, MS Excel and MS PowerPoint;

• Writing ability in both Bangla and English format
